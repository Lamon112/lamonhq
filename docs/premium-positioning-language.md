# Premium Positioning Language — lexical swap blueprint
*Lamon Agency / Plima · v1 · 2026-05-11*

> **Why this document exists:** Premium positioning isn't only what you sell — it's the words you use to describe selling. 9 lexical swaps that signal authority, expertise, and selectiveness instead of subordination, commodity, and desperation. Apply across email, DM, sales calls, scripts, social copy.

---

## TL;DR — 5 meta-principles

Every swap below maps to one of these 5 underlying patterns:

| # | Principle | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Authority over permission** | You lead the recommendation, not ask for buy-in |
| 2 | **Value over price** | Frame around investment and outcome, not cost |
| 3 | **Expertise over commodity** | Specialist by result, not general by tenure |
| 4 | **Gracious not apologetic** | Subordination-free etiquette |
| 5 | **Selective not desperate** | Implied calendar, not begging for time |

## The 9 swaps — with HR/CRO adaptation

### 1 · LEAD don't ASK
**Principle:** Authority over permission

| ❌ Avoid | ✅ Use |
|---|---|
| EN: "Does this work for you?" | EN: "Here's what I recommend." |
| HR: *"Odgovara li vam ovo?"* | HR: *"Evo što preporučam."* |

**Why it lands:** "Does this work" puts client in driver's seat as approver. "Here's what I recommend" puts you in driver's seat as expert. Premium clients hire experts, not order-takers.

**Use in:** Discovery call recommendations · Proposal framing · Slack/email when suggesting next step

**Anti-edge:** Don't pivot to dictatorial. "I recommend X *because of Y*" — always ground recommendation in their context.

---

### 2 · INVESTMENT framing
**Principle:** Value over price

| ❌ Avoid | ✅ Use |
|---|---|
| EN: "Why prices start at $X" | EN: "The investment begins at $X" |
| HR: *"Cijene počinju od €X"* | HR: *"Investicija započinje sa €X"* |

**Why it lands:** "Cijena" ⇒ sunk cost; psychological pain. "Investicija" ⇒ future return; psychological gain. Same number, different mental category.

**Use in:** Tier comparison page · Sales deck pricing slide · Email proposal · Discovery call closing

**Anti-edge:** Don't fake-frame trivial things as investment ("investicija u kavu"). Reserve for things with measurable ROI.

---

### 3 · SPECIALIST language
**Principle:** Expertise over commodity

| ❌ Avoid | ✅ Use |
|---|---|
| EN: "I just do dental marketing" | EN: "I specialize in premium clinic growth systems." |
| HR: *"Radim samo dental marketing"* | HR: *"Specijaliziran sam za premium klinika growth sustave."* |

**Why it lands:** "Just do" diminishes (you do A and only A — nothing more). "Specialize" elevates (you do A *deeply*, by design, as choice). Specialist commands 3-10× pricing of generalist.

**Use in:** LinkedIn bio · IG bio · Discovery call self-intro · Email signature · Sales pitch opening

**HR specifics:** Consider also *"Radim isključivo s..."* (work exclusively with) as a softer Croatian variant when "specijaliziran" feels too clinical.

**Anti-edge:** "Specialize" requires actual specialization. Don't claim it if you serve any vertical that pays.

---

### 4 · THE STANDARD frame
**Principle:** Authority over permission

| ❌ Avoid | ✅ Use |
|---|---|
| EN: "Stop talking like you need clients" | EN: "Talk like you ARE the standard." |
| HR: *"Prestani govoriti kao da trebaš klijente"* | HR: *"Govori kao da si ti standard."* |

**Why it lands:** Need-energy repels premium clients (they sense desperation). Standard-energy attracts (they want to align with the category leader). Same person, opposite outcomes.

**Use in:** All copy (subtle, always-on filter) · Sales call energy · Refusing scope creep · Replying to objections

**Anti-edge:** Don't conflate confidence with arrogance. "I am the standard" ≠ "I am better than you." Standard means "this is how it's done in this category."

---

### 5 · OUTCOME not TENURE
**Principle:** Expertise over commodity

| ❌ Avoid | ✅ Use |
|---|---|
| EN: "I've been doing this 10 years" | EN: "I've built my entire practice around getting this specific result." |
| HR: *"Radim ovo 10 godina"* | HR: *"Izgradio sam cijelu agenciju oko ovog specifičnog ishoda."* |

**Why it lands:** Tenure = years (commodity attribute, anyone can rack up time). Outcome = mastery of a specific result (rare, defensible, premium signal). Premium clients hire for the *result*, not the *years*.

**Use in:** Bio · Discovery call self-intro · Case study openings · Sales deck "About me" slide · LinkedIn About section

**Anti-edge:** You actually need a specific result. "I've built my practice around getting [vague benefit]" defeats the move. Be concrete: *"...around getting premium clinics from referral-only to systematic 30%+ growth in 6 months."*

---

### 6 · PACKAGES-first not BUDGET-first
**Principle:** Authority over permission · Value over price

| ❌ Avoid | ✅ Use |
|---|---|
| EN: "What's your budget?" | EN: "Let me walk you through my packages." |
| HR: *"Koji je vaš budget?"* | HR: *"Dajte da vas provedem kroz svoje pakete."* |

**Why it lands:** "What's your budget" cedes control + reveals you'll customize/discount to whatever number they say. "Let me walk you through my packages" implies *you have a defined product*; client picks tier that matches their reality. Plus: tier anchoring (Mreža/Voice/Premium) shifts decision from "should we?" to "which tier?".

**Use in:** Discovery calls (always) · Inbound DM replies · Email proposals · Pricing-related objection handling

**Anti-edge:** Have actual packages defined. If you're still custom-everything, this swap will sound disconnected. (Plima already has 3-tier.)

---

### 7 · GRATITUDE not APOLOGY
**Principle:** Gracious not apologetic

| ❌ Avoid | ✅ Use |
|---|---|
| EN: "Sorry for the late response" | EN: "Thank you for your patience." |
| HR: *"Oprostite na kasnoj reakciji"* | HR: *"Hvala vam na strpljenju."* |

**Why it lands:** Apology = "I did wrong, please forgive." Gratitude = "You did right by waiting, I appreciate." Same lateness, opposite emotional charge. Apology positions you below; gratitude positions you alongside.

**Use in:** Every email/DM where you're responding late · Slack replies · Calendly reschedule notes · Voicemail callbacks

**Anti-edge:** Don't use this when you genuinely owed an apology (missed commitment, broke promise). "Hvala na strpljenju" then sounds like deflection. Reserve for delays that aren't your fault or weren't promised.

---

### 8 · AVAILABILITY signaling
**Principle:** Selective not desperate

| ❌ Avoid | ✅ Use |
|---|---|
| EN: "I can try to fit you in" | EN: "Let me check my availability." |
| HR: *"Pokušat ću vas ubaciti"* | HR: *"Da provjerim svoju dostupnost."* |

**Why it lands:** "Try to fit you in" = your calendar has free slots, you're rearranging to accommodate. "Check my availability" = your calendar is structured, you have system, slot must be allocated. Premium clients want providers whose time is structured (signals other premium clients exist).

**Use in:** Inbound discovery requests · Reschedule conversations · "Can we talk this week?" replies · Call confirmations

**Anti-edge:** Don't fake-busy. "Let me check my availability" then immediately replying "Tuesday 2pm works" is fine. Don't manufacture artificial 3-day delays — that's fake-scarcity and clients can smell it.

---

### 9 · BONUS pattern: Imply, don't claim

This isn't one of the 9 explicit swaps but it's the meta-rhythm running through them. Premium positioning *implies* status; commodity positioning *claims* it.

| ❌ Claims | ✅ Implies |
|---|---|
| "We're the best dental marketing agency in Croatia." | "Plima is the standard for premium private clinics." |
| "Our clients see 5x ROI." | "Clinics that ran Plima for 6+ months..." (let the data tell) |
| "I'm an expert in conversion optimization." | "I built my agency around getting clinics from 40% to 70% booking conversion." |

**Use in:** All copy without exception. If you find yourself writing "we are the best X," delete it and find a way to *show* it.

## Self-audit checklist (read before sending any client-facing copy)

- [ ] Da li tražim permission ili dajem recommendation? (#1)
- [ ] Da li sam rekao "cijena" ili "investicija"? (#2)
- [ ] Da li sam rekao "samo radim X" ili "specijaliziran sam za X"? (#3)
- [ ] Da li zvučim kao da trebam klijenta ili kao da sam standard? (#4)
- [ ] Da li sam istakao tenure ili specifičan outcome? (#5)
- [ ] Da li sam pitao "koji budget?" ili predstavio packages? (#6)
- [ ] Da li sam se ispričao kad nisam morao (#7)
- [ ] Da li sam rekao "pokušat ću ubaciti" ili "provjerit ću dostupnost"? (#8)
- [ ] Da li *implicira* status ili *tvrdim* status? (#9)

Ako 8+ od 9 prošla — copy je premium-aligned. Ako <6 prošla — prepisati.
